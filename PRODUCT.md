# Splitbill

## Product register

**Surface:** product (utility app UI — not marketing)

**Audience:** Friends at a restaurant or shared meal who need to split a receipt fairly on one phone, outdoors or in dim lighting, often one-handed.

**Job to be done:** Photograph a receipt, correct the line items, assign who shared each dish, and see clear per-person totals including tax and tip — without charging people for food they did not eat.

**Voice:** Calm, clear, trustworthy. Short labels. Numbers are the hero. No playful slang that obscures money.

**Anti-references:** Purple SaaS gradients, Inter/Roboto defaults, nested cards, dashboard chrome, floating promo badges, dark neon glow.

**Constraints:** Static PWA, no server, on-device OCR, mobile-first, installable, works offline after first load.
